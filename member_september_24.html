healthy shopping on a budget

Healthy eating can be hard for some kids—and some adults! You can help your child develop good eating habits by choosing nutrient-rich foods at the grocery store. Here are some ideas to find healthy and affordable food on your next shopping trip:

• Plan ahead so you know when items are on sale.
• Make a shopping list and stick to it. Be sure to include healthy foods like vegetables, fruits, lean protein, and whole grains.
• Canned and frozen foods cost less and are healthy. Look for items with less salt.
• Avoid waste by eating fresh food soon after you buy it.
• Be sure to buy healthy foods that your kids like to eat.

With childhood obesity on the rise, there’s no better time to model healthy habits for your kids.

Learn quick, healthy ways to cook the food from your shopping trip.

find a provider who speaks your language.

It’s important to feel comfortable speaking to your primary care provider (PCP) about any health concerns. If Spanish is your first language, you can find a PCP who speaks it. Here’s how:

1. Use our online Find a Provider tool.
2. Enter your preferred location, then click Select your plan.
3. A dropdown menu will appear. Click on MeridianHealth: Medicaid, then click Continue.
4. Under Choose a Category, click Medical Professionals, then Primary Care, and select a specialty such as Family Medicine. A list of providers will appear.
5. On the left of this page under Filter Options, click Additional Office Languages, select Spanish, then click Apply. A list of Spanish‑speaking providers in your area will appear.

Remember, you can change your PCP at any time.

encuentre un proveedor que hable su idioma

Es importante que se sienta a gusto hablando con su proveedor de atención primaria (PCP) sobre cualquier problema de salud. Si el español es su lengua materna, puede encontrar un PCP que lo hable. Aquí le explicamos cómo:

1. Utilice la herramienta en línea Buscar un proveedor.
2. Ingrese su ubicación de preferencia y, a continuación, haga clic en Seleccione su plan.
3. Aparecerá un menú desplegable. Haga clic en MeridianHealth: Medicaid y, luego, haga clic en Continuar.
4. En Elija una categoría, haga clic en Profesionales médicos y, a continuación, en Atención primaria, y seleccione una especialidad como Medicina familiar. Aparecerá una lista de proveedores.
5. A la izquierda de esta página, en Opciones de filtro, haga clic en Idiomas adicionales del consultorio, seleccione Spanish (Español) y, a continuación, haga clic en Aplicar. Aparecerá una lista de proveedores de habla hispana en su zona.

Puede cambiar de PCP en cualquier momento siguiendo los pasos anteriores.
